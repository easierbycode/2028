We're here to help you with any issues or questions.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I play? Use touch controls or keyboard arrows to move your ship and fire at the invading enemies. Survive waves of increasingly difficult enemies and defeat bosses.
  • The game won't load. Make sure you have a stable internet connection. Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. The game works best on modern browsers (Chrome, Safari, Firefox).
  • Controls aren't responding. Ensure no other overlays or browser extensions are interfering. On mobile, use the on-screen touch controls.
  • How do I report a bug? Please contact us using the information below, or open an issue on our GitHub repository.

Device Requirements

  • iOS 14.0 or later / Android 8.0 or later
  • A modern web browser with JavaScript enabled
  • Stable internet connection for initial load

Privacy

2048.ai does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The game may use local storage to save your preferences and high scores on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers beyond what is required to load the game. For leaderboard functionality, only your chosen display name and score are stored.
